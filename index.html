Nearby Attraction

Just an hour's drive from Ahmedabad, the Nalsarovar Bird Sanctuary spans over 120 square kilometers, providing a vibrant habitat for over 250 bird species, including migratory spectacles like flamingos and cranes. This birdwatcher's paradise is best visited during the winter months when these species are most abundant. Nearby, the historic site of Lothal offers insights into the ancient Indus Valley Civilization, making it a perfect complement to the natural beauty of Nalsarovar. Together, these attractions form a rich tapestry of nature and history, ideal for exploring from the Visamo Nalsarovar Resort.
